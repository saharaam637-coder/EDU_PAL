Our Mission:
To streamline classroom management by giving teachers simple tools to track topics, students, and schedules. We believe technology should reduce workload, not add complexity.
Our Vision
To build an intuitive educational companion that reduces workload and improves organization for educators everywhere, helping them focus on what matters most - teaching.
Why We Selected This Project:
• Inspired by challenges faced during online learning in COVID-19, when teachers were under pressure to grade assignments quickly and maintain curriculum.
• Our goal is to streamline and simplify the teaching process using intuitive digital tools.
• We wanted to create something that could make a real difference in educators' daily lives.
Intended Audience:
• Teachers from public schools and online learning organizations (e.g., Kumon, virtual schools).
• Education coordinators and curriculum managers.
• Tutors and private instructors.
What the App Does:
• Helps teachers organize lesson materials and classrooms.
• Provides topic tracking, student insights, and calendar tools.
• Makes weekly lesson planning easier and more consistent.
Core Use Case:
• Reduce teacher workload by simplifying organization and classroom tracking.
• Improve remote and hybrid learning outcomes.
• Allow flexible weekly lesson adjustments.
